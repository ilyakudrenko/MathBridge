About Core School

Core School is a focused online tutoring platform built by educators who believe that the right guidance makes the difference. Our mission is to make learning clear, structured, and confidence-building.

Whether you’re preparing for exams, catching up on schoolwork, or aiming to get ahead, our tutors create personalized paths in each subject to fit your needs.

Why we built Core School: we wanted a place where students could find expert tutors without the clutter — simple subjects, clear pricing, and real 1-on-1 sessions.

What subjects do you offer?

We offer Mathematics, Biology, Artificial Intelligence, and Computer Science. Each subject has dedicated tutors you can browse and book.

How do I book a lesson?

Choose a subject from the home page or Classes section, pick a tutor on the class page, then select a time on their schedule and click Enroll. You need an account and credits to complete booking.

How does credit pricing work?

Sessions are paid with credits. You buy credit packs (e.g. 45 credits for 45 USD); each lesson costs credits based on subject and duration. See packs and pricing.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes. If you cancel at least 24 hours before the session, you get a full credit refund. Cancellations within 24 hours receive a 50% refund. You can manage your bookings from your profile.

How do I contact support?

Email us at contact@coreschool.com or see Contact us above to book a short intro call with our team.
