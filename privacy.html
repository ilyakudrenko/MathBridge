Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Core School (“we”, “us”) operates this tutoring platform.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide when you create an account, complete your profile, purchase credits, book sessions, or contact us. This may include:

  • Name, email address, phone number (optional), and profile photo if you upload one
  • Account credentials (passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing; we never store plain-text passwords)
  • Booking and enrollment details, messages related to sessions, and payment-related records processed by our payment provider
  • For tutors: subjects taught, Calendly or scheduling links you choose to share, and content you add to your public profile

2. How we use your information

We use this information to operate the platform, authenticate users, match students with tutors, process bookings and credits, send essential service emails (e.g. password reset, booking confirmations where enabled), improve security, and comply with law.

3. Sharing and processors

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers who help us run the site (for example: hosting, email delivery, payment processing, analytics) under contracts that limit their use of your data. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

4. Cookies and local storage

We may use cookies or similar technologies where needed for login sessions and site functionality. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data retention

We retain account and transaction-related information for as long as your account is active and as needed for legal, tax, and dispute-resolution purposes. You may request deletion of your account subject to applicable exceptions.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure; we encourage you to use a strong, unique password.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, or to object to certain processing. Contact us using the details below to make a request.

8. Children

Our services are intended for users who can lawfully enter into contracts. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us.

9. International users

If you access the service from outside the country where our servers or providers are located, your data may be transferred and processed in those jurisdictions.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and adjust the “Last updated” date.

11. Contact

Questions about privacy: contact@coreschool.com
